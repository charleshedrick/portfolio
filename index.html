Hello! I'm a Jacksonville-based Frontend Web Developer and Designer on a mission to bring the best of both worlds to the digital realm. With over a decade of design and fine art experience under my belt, I blend creativity and technical prowess to craft visually stunning and user-friendly websites. With two years of web development experience, I'm constantly learning new technologies and pushing the boundaries of my skills. Beyond the screen, you'll find me indulging in my love for art and soaking up the beauty of nature on a local trail. I'm passionate about good design and creating memorable user experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Visual Art & Design

My background in visual art and design has instilled in me a keen eye for aesthetics, composition, and user-centered design principles. I approach web development with a deep appreciation for the marriage of form and function, crafting websites that not only function seamlessly but also delight the eyes.

Web Development

My journey into web development has been an exciting one. I've dived headfirst into learning essential technologies such as vanilla JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, and responsive design. These skills empower me to create dynamic, interactive, and mobile-friendly websites that engage users across platforms.

Design Tools

Proficient in design tools like Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, Procreate and Blender, I am able to conceptualize, prototype, and refine user interfaces. My design skills ensure that every web project I undertake is visually appealing and user-friendly.

Continuous Learning

I believe that learning is a lifelong journey. I am committed to staying at the forefront of web development by continually expanding my knowledge and exploring emerging technologies and languages. I am currently learning more PHP for a blog website with login capabilities.

Portfolio

My portfolio showcases the fusion of my visual artistry and web development prowess, resulting in unique, responsive, and visually stunning web experiences. I'm excited to bring my skills and passion to your next project.
